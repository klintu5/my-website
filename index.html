The room

Seattle polish without the hush.

Harshithings is built for crisp martinis, cedar smoke, quiet confidence, and the kind of service that knows when to appear. The dining room pairs stone, walnut, and low amber light with views inspired by Elliott Bay.

Dry-aged program

Steaks selected for depth, not spectacle.

Our beef program favors balanced marbling, mineral character, and patient aging. Cuts are butchered daily, rested properly, and served with sauces that support the steak instead of stealing the table.
